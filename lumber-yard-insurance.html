Common Questions

Lumber Yard Insurance FAQ

What does lumber yard insurance cover?

Typically property and inventory, general liability for customers and operations, commercial auto for delivery, and workers' comp — we review your whole program for gaps and savings.

Is my delivery fleet covered?

Commercial auto covers your delivery trucks and boom trucks. We confirm your fleet and hauling exposure are properly insured.

How is my lumber inventory valued?

Inventory values can swing with lumber prices. We help make sure your property and inventory limits reflect real replacement values so you're not underinsured.

Can you help with customer liability claims exposure?

Yes — foot traffic, loading, and delivery all create liability exposure. We review your general liability limits against how your yard actually operates.
