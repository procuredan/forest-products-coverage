Common Questions

Sawmill Insurance FAQ

Why does sawmill insurance focus so much on fire?

Sawdust, wood storage, and kilns create significant fire load, so carriers weigh fire protection heavily. Good housekeeping and the right carrier can meaningfully lower your premium.

Do I need products liability?

If you manufacture and sell lumber or wood products, products liability protects against claims tied to the products you produce. We review whether your limits fit your output.

Is business interruption worth adding?

For mills, a fire or breakdown can halt production for months. Business interruption coverage replaces lost income — we review whether it fits your operation.

Is mill machinery covered if it breaks down?

Equipment breakdown coverage can apply to mill machinery. We confirm what's covered and compare carriers that write it competitively.
